The Texas Oil Boom

The Birth of Texas oil

On August 27, 1859, George H. Bissell and his friends struck oil at a depth of 69 feet near Titusville. This is the first recorded time that oil was struck, and this started the birth of the modern oil industry.

After this, towns around Titusville and areas around the U.S. struck oil too, which made everyone start looking for oil. All of the U.S. began searching for oil to gain massive amounts of money for themselves.

History of Oil in Texas

Oil was first discovered by the native americans who used it for waterproofing of boats and other things. Later on however americans discovered that oil could be used for lighting machines and other very important things.
